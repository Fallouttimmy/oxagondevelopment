§ 01

ROW Policy — Rights of Origin & Watermark Integrity

1.1 Ownership and Origin

All content, software, designs, media, documentation, branding elements, and derivative works created, distributed, or authorized under the Organization are protected intellectual property. A ROW identifier constitutes proof of origin and ownership.

1.2 Integrity Protection

The following actions are prohibited without explicit authorization:

  • Removal or alteration of ROW identifiers
  • Obscuring or covering watermarks
  • Redistributing modified protected content without permission
  • Claiming ownership of protected material
  • Attempting to bypass watermark protections

Circumvention of ROW protections constitutes unauthorized modification of protected property regardless of medium or intent.

1.3 Enforcement Authority

The Organization reserves the right to issue takedown requests, restrict access, suspend or terminate accounts, revoke permissions, and apply network-wide bans.

1.4 Persistence Clause

ROW protections remain in effect permanently, including after a user leaves any community or service.

§ 02

Privacy Policy — Minimal Data & Human Verification Model

2.1 Limited Data Storage

The Organization does not collect or store personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to operate accounts. Stored information may include account identifiers, login credentials, and basic operational records. No unnecessary tracking or profiling is performed.

2.2 Verification Data Handling

If verification requires face images, identification documents, or similar materials: data is used only for one-time verification, is not permanently stored, is deleted immediately after verification, and no biometric database is created.

2.3 Human Review Guarantee

Verification processes are performed by authorized human reviewers, not automated AI decision systems. Automation may assist technically, but final decisions are made by humans.

2.4 Temporary Processing Clause

Sensitive materials exist only temporarily during processing and are removed after verification within a reasonable timeframe.

2.5 No Sale or Sharing

The Organization does not sell or trade personal data. Data may only be shared when required for service operation, required by law, or authorized by the user.

2.6 Security Disclaimer

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

§ 03

Automatic Agreement Policy — Binding Acceptance

3.1 Conditions of Acceptance

A person is considered to have agreed if they join an OSN server or community, use any application or service, create an account, or continue use after policies are presented. Participation constitutes acceptance without a written signature.

3.2 Scope of Agreement

Policies apply across all Organization platforms, future updates, associated systems, and interactions with other agreeing members.

3.3 Continuing Obligation

Obligations remain enforceable after leaving, deleting accounts, or discontinuing use.

§ 04

OSN Conduct & Community Protection Policy

4.1 Respect and Safety

Users must not engage in harassment, bullying, threats, discrimination, or targeted attacks.

4.2 External Harm Policy

Users who agreed to policies must not create or distribute content intended to harm, harass, or maliciously target other agreeing members, staff or administrators, or the Organization or its projects. This applies even outside Organization platforms when related to the community.

4.3 Media and Video Restrictions

Prohibited content includes:

  • Misleading or manipulated media intended to cause harm
  • False allegations presented as fact
  • Exposure of private information
  • Coordinated harassment

Legitimate criticism is allowed if non-abusive and non-defamatory.

4.4 Anti-Exploitation Rule

Users must not exploit vulnerabilities, bypass safeguards, or abuse permissions.

§ 05

Enforcement Framework

Violations may result in the following actions, with severity determining enforcement level:

Warning Restrictions Content Removal Temporary Suspension Permanent Ban Account Termination Network-Wide Revocation
§ 06

Policy Supremacy Clause

If conflicts exist, the interpretation providing greater protection to safety, organizational integrity, intellectual property, and community stability shall prevail.

§ 07

Modification Rights

Policies may be updated at any time. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

§ 08

Severability

If any clause is found invalid or unenforceable, all remaining provisions remain fully effective.

§ 09

Good-Faith Principle

Users agree not to exploit loopholes or ambiguities. Attempts to bypass rules through technicalities may still be treated as violations at the Organization's discretion.
